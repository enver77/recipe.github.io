Chicken Burrito Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Large flour tortillas

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken, cooked rice, black beans, cheddar cheese, salsa, sour cream, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

2. Place a large spoonful of the chicken mixture in the center of each tortilla.

3. Fold in the sides and roll up the tortillas to form burritos.

4. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Chicken Burritos!
