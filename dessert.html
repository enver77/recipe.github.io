Chocolate Brownie Delight

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, melted butter, cocoa powder, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Stir in chopped nuts and chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  6. Cool before cutting into squares. Enjoy your Chocolate Brownie Delight!

Calories: 300 per serving

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Classic New York Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 4 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C) and grease a springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press into the bottom of the prepared pan to create the crust.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in sour cream, flour, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the pan.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is set and the top is lightly browned.
  7. Cool in the pan on a wire rack, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving. Enjoy your Classic New York Cheesecake!

Calories: 250 per serving

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Baklava

Ingredients:

  • 1 package phyllo dough, thawed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 cups finely chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, combine chopped walnuts, sugar, and ground cinnamon for the filling.
  3. Place one sheet of phyllo dough in the prepared dish, brush with melted butter, and repeat until you have 8-10 layers.
  4. Sprinkle a portion of the nut mixture over the phyllo layers.
  5. Repeat the layering process with phyllo and nuts until all ingredients are used, ending with a top layer of phyllo.
  6. Cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes using a sharp knife.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.
  8. While baking, prepare the syrup by boiling water, honey, and vanilla extract. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Once the baklava is out of the oven, pour the hot syrup over the hot baklava. Allow it to cool and absorb the syrup before serving. Enjoy your Baklava!

Calories: 500 per serving

Prep Time: 100 minutes
